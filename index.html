What is the Finance Bill?

Every year, Kenya's National Treasury tables a Finance Bill — a proposed law that changes how the government collects taxes. It's how Parliament decides what you pay more of, what gets cheaper, and what new taxes are introduced.

The Finance Bill 2026 (National Assembly Bill No. 26 of 2026) was released on 30 April 2026. If passed, most changes take effect on 1 July 2026. You have the right to comment before it becomes law.

Kiswahili

Muswada wa Fedha ni nini?

Kila mwaka, Wizara ya Fedha huleta Muswada wa Fedha — sheria inayopendekeza mabadiliko ya jinsi serikali inavyokusanya kodi. Ni njia ambayo Bunge hufanya maamuzi kuhusu nini kitaongezwa kodi, nini kitapunguzwa, na kodi gani mpya zitaanzishwa.

Muswada wa Fedha 2026 ulitolewa tarehe 30 Aprili 2026. Ukipitishwa, mabadiliko mengi yataanza kutumika tarehe 1 Julai 2026. Una haki ya kutoa maoni yako kabla haujawa sheria.
